About Me 5/1/2025

Hey there, I’m Liz. I designed and coded this site and recorded all of these videos myself. I’m an undergraduate student studying Information Systems and Analytics, and throughout my coursework I’ve developed a real love for coding — SQL in particular.

I’m passionate about providing accessible educational content for everyone, regardless of their background or financial situation. My goal is to make learning feel less intimidating and more approachable. Through creating free, in-depth coding tutorials, I hope anyone who’s curious and motivated to learn has access to high-quality resources.
